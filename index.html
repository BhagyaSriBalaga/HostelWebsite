Facilities

Our dormitory-style bedrooms are designed with your comfort and convenience in mind.

In addition to our shared dormitories, we offer cozy private rooms for guests who prefer a bit more privacy during their stay.

Our friendly staff are here for you around the clock, ready to assist with anything you need, from check-in to booking excursions and beyond.

Our inviting dining hall is the heart of our hostel, where guests gather to enjoy delicious meals and connect with fellow travelers.

Pack light and make use of our convenient laundry facilities, equipped with washing machines and dryers, so you can keep your clothes clean and fresh throughout your journey.

Stay connected with friends and family back home or plan your next adventure with our complimentary Wi-Fi available throughout the hostel.

Room Allocation

In addition to our dormitory-style bedrooms, we offer cozy 3-bedded rooms for guests who prefer a bit more privacy while still enjoying the camaraderie of shared accommodations.

There are usually two single beds or a double bed in the room, allowing two individuals to sleep comfortably.

Having your own room allows you to spread out and relax without having to share your space with others.
